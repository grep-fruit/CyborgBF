about :

Vert Partout is a montreal/tio:tia'ke-based artist, who splits his time between community, poetry & art, and spending too much time on the computer. He makes images (and all types of trinkets that you can stick on your fridge), and writes poetry (...yea).

His themes include :

  • fiction (its impossibility, its inevitability)
  • creation as a form of collage
  • cyborgs and bodies (organic, mechanical, chemical)
  • emo memories
  • the superior efficiency of forgetting over forgiving
  • men and (trans)masculinity (but like, in a gay way)

Poetry is not serious and is quite embarrassing.

Can someone come pick him up? He's once again stuck at the intersection of art and technology.

For any question, information, order, special request, invitation, send me a message!
